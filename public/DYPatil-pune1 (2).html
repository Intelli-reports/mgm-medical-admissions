🏛️ About Dr. D.Y. Patil Medical College, Pune

Dr. D.Y. Patil Medical College, Pune, established in 1996, is the flagship campus of Dr. D.Y. Patil Vidyapeeth (Deemed to be University). Located in Pimpri, Pune, it is one of the most sought-after medical colleges in Maharashtra. The institution is recognized by the National Medical Commission (NMC) and accredited with NAAC A++ grade.

With a 1700+ bedded teaching hospital, the college provides extensive clinical exposure to students. The campus houses modern lecture theaters, advanced laboratories, a central library with over 50,000 books, and research centers. The institution has a strong alumni network and is known for its research output and global collaborations.

📝 Admission Process

Admission to MBBS is through NEET-UG followed by centralized counseling by MCC (Medical Counselling Committee) for All India Quota and State DME for Maharashtra State Quota. PG admissions are through NEET-PG counseling. NRI/Management quota seats are available as per university norms and government regulations.

📄 Required Documents

  • NEET Score Card / Rank Letter
  • 10th & 12th Marksheets (Duly attested)
  • School Leaving Certificate / Transfer Certificate
  • Migration Certificate (if applicable)
  • Caste Certificate (for reserved categories)
  • 8 Passport size photographs
  • Aadhar Card / Valid ID Proof

❓ Frequently Asked Questions – Dr. D.Y. Patil Medical College, Pune

What is the MBBS fee at Dr. D.Y. Patil Medical College, Pune?▼

How many MBBS seats are available at DY Patil Pune?▼

Is DY Patil Medical College Pune NMC approved?▼
Yes, Dr. D.Y. Patil Medical College, Pune is fully approved by the National Medical Commission (NMC) and affiliated to Dr. D.Y. Patil Vidyapeeth, Pune. It holds NAAC A++ grade accreditation.
