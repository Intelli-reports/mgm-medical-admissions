🏛️ About MGM Medical College, Vashi

MGM Medical College, Vashi, established in 1995, is a constituent college of MGM Institute of Health Sciences (Deemed to be University). Located in the heart of Navi Mumbai, the college is recognized by the National Medical Commission (NMC) and accredited with NAAC A Grade. The attached MGM Hospital is a 700+ bedded multispecialty facility providing exceptional clinical training.

The campus boasts modern infrastructure, smart classrooms, a well-stocked digital library, and advanced simulation labs. With a focus on holistic education, MGM Vashi prepares students for global healthcare challenges.

📝 Admission Process

Admission to MBBS is through NEET-UG followed by centralized counseling by MCC (Medical Counselling Committee) for All India Quota and State DME for Maharashtra State Quota. PG admissions are through NEET-PG counseling.

📄 Required Documents

  • NEET Score Card / Rank Letter
  • 10th & 12th Marksheets (Duly attested)
  • School Leaving Certificate / Transfer Certificate
  • Migration Certificate (if applicable)
  • Caste Certificate (for reserved categories)
  • 8 Passport size photographs
  • Aadhar Card / Valid ID Proof

❓ Frequently Asked Questions – MGM Medical College, Vashi

What is the MBBS fee at MGM Medical College, Vashi?▼
The annual MBBS fee at MGM Medical College Vashi is ₹20,00,000. NRI quota annual fee is ₹45,00,000. Fees are subject to revision as per university norms.
What is the NEET cutoff rank for MBBS at MGM Vashi?▼
For General category, the expected closing rank (AIQ) is around 18,500. OBC: ~24,500 | SC: ~55,000 | ST: ~70,000. Ranks are indicative based on previous year data.
How many MBBS seats are available at MGM Vashi?▼
MGM Medical College, Vashi has a total MBBS intake of 150 seats per year, approved by the National Medical Commission (NMC).
Is MGM Medical College Vashi NMC approved?▼
Yes, MGM Medical College, Vashi is fully approved by the National Medical Commission (NMC) and affiliated to MGM Institute of Health Sciences (Deemed University). It holds NAAC 'A' Grade accreditation.
Does MGM Vashi have hostel facility?▼
Yes, MGM Vashi provides separate AC and Non-AC hostels for boys and girls. There is also a dedicated international hostel for NRI students on campus.
How to apply for NRI quota at MGM Medical College Vashi?▼
NRI quota admissions are managed directly by the institution. Candidates must be NRI/OCI/PIO or sponsored by NRI parents. NEET qualification is mandatory. Contact the admission office at +91 22 2767 1650.
What is the hospital attached to MGM Medical College Vashi?▼
