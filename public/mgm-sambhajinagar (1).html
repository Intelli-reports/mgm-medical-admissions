🏛️ About MGM Medical College, Sambhaji Nagar

MGM Medical College, Sambhaji Nagar (formerly Aurangabad), established in 1990, is a constituent college of MGM Institute of Health Sciences (Deemed to be University). It is located in the historic city of Sambhaji Nagar (Aurangabad) in Maharashtra and is recognized by the National Medical Commission (NMC). The college is accredited with NAAC A+ grade.

The attached MGM Hospital is a 1500+ bedded multispecialty hospital providing excellent clinical training. The campus features modern infrastructure, digital library, research labs, and separate hostels for boys and girls.

📝 Admission Process

Admission to MBBS is through NEET-UG followed by centralized counseling by MCC (Medical Counselling Committee) for All India Quota and State DME for Maharashtra State Quota. PG admissions are through NEET-PG counseling.

📄 Required Documents

  • NEET Score Card / Rank Letter
  • 10th & 12th Marksheets (Duly attested)
  • School Leaving Certificate / Transfer Certificate
  • Migration Certificate (if applicable)
  • Caste Certificate (for reserved categories)
  • 8 Passport size photographs
  • Aadhar Card / Valid ID Proof
